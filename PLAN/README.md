## The One Line

## The Honest Read

This is a Clanker token launch. The product is real (cycles ship, scams are blocked, receipts exist) but the monetization is a token. The plan optimizes both: a credible product story so the token has a floor, and a fee structure that doesn't depend on holders not dumping.

The single biggest fee-mechanic decision — take fees in **WETH (Paired)** not in **$ORBIT** — is locked in `DECISIONS.md` and detailed in `CLANKER_FEE_STRATEGY.md`. This is non-negotiable.

## What This Plan Optimizes For

In priority order:

1. **Sustainable treasury cash flow** (trading-fee capture in WETH)
2. **Visible daily activity** (cycle proofs, casts, receipts)
3. **Trust artifact credibility** (signed proofs, verifier, public dashboard)
4. **Adoption beyond the genesis repo** (SDK, scaffolder, registry)
5. **Cultural durability** (lore, voice, anniversary cycles)

What it does NOT optimize for: enterprise procurement, compliance certs, ZK trust layer, smart-account execution. Those are explicitly deferred to year 2+.
